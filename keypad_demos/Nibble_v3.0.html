How to Use:

Click/Tap: Press buttons to add their values together. Release to confirm the sum.

Example: Press and hold buttons 2 and 3, then release to enter 6.

Correct code: 3, 10, 7, 8

- Press buttons 1 and 3 together, release to enter 3

- Press buttons 2 and 3 together, release to enter 10

- Press button 1, 2 and 3 together, release to enter 7

- Press button 4, release to enter 8

Watch the LEDs for system status feedback!
